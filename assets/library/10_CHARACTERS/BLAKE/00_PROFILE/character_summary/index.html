    Character Summary — Blake

    Core Identity

    Name: Blake

    Height: 191 cm (6'3")

    Build Type: athletic_muscular — large athletic build with strong upper-body emphasis

    Age Appearance: late twenties / around 30

    Gender Presentation: masculine, confident athletic

    Visual Identity (Short Prompt Version)

    Use this block when a prompt needs a short character description.

    Blake is a 6'3" tall, broad-shouldered man with a muscular athletic build, strong chest and arms, and a confident masculine presence.
    His aesthetic is polished athletic style with fitted clothing, dark colors, and understated luxury.

    Visual Identity (Full Prompt Version)

    Use this block when a prompt needs a complete character description.

    Blake is 6'3" with a tall, powerful athletic build and strong upper-body development.

    Key features:

    • face shape: square face with strong structure
    • jawline: defined jawline with a masculine edge
    • eyes: calm, confident eyes with a focused expression
    • hair: short dark brown hair styled cleanly with a natural athletic look
    • skin tone: light skin with a warm natural tone

    Body proportions:

    • shoulder width: broad shoulders with strong upper-body presence
    • torso shape: muscular torso with defined chest and solid build
    • limb proportions: thick arms and strong legs with athletic definition
    • posture: upright and confident with relaxed control

    Overall silhouette:

    Blake has a tall, broad, upper-body-dominant silhouette. His wide shoulders, thick arms, and muscular chest create a powerful athletic presence that reads stable, grounded, and physically imposing.

    Style Identity

    Aesthetic keywords:

    • athletic
    • luxury
    • streetwear

    Typical clothing elements:

    • fitted athletic shirts or tanks
    • tapered joggers or athletic shorts
    • structured jackets or coats

    Typical materials:

    • cotton
    • mesh
    • wool

    Typical colors:

    Primary colors:

    • black
    • charcoal
    • navy
    • olive

    Accent colors:

    • white

    Accessories

    Recurring items that often appear with the character:

    • athletic trainers
    • casual sneakers
    • minimal watch

    Body Language

    Posture:

    Relaxed but upright posture with a grounded, confident stance.

    Movement style:

    Strong, deliberate movement with controlled physical presence.

    Gesture style:

    Confident and open gestures, often relaxed but assertive.

    Expression Profile

    Default expression:

    Confident neutral expression with calm focus.

    Typical smile:

    Warm, slightly amused smile that appears easily in relaxed moments.

    Emotional range:

    Controlled but genuine emotional presence; confident, steady, and reassuring.

    Personality Snapshot

    Short personality description used for scene prompts.

    Blake is confident, steady, and quietly charismatic. He carries himself with calm assurance and natural leadership energy. His presence feels grounded and protective, balancing strength with a relaxed and approachable demeanor.

    Identity Preservation Rules

    These traits should never drift in generated images.

    Always preserve:

    • height around 6'3"
    • tall, muscular athletic build
    • broad shoulders and strong chest
    • thick arms and powerful physique
    • short dark brown hair
    • confident masculine presence
    • fitted athletic clothing style

    Avoid:

    • slim or runner-style build
    • narrow shoulders
    • delicate or lightweight physique
    • short stature
    • overly flashy clothing or bright palettes
