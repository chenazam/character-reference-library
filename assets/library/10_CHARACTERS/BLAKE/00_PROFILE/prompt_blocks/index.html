    Prompt Blocks — Blake

    This file contains reusable prompt fragments describing the character. Each block can be copied into prompts depending on the generation task.

    Character Block

    Use for:

    • reference sheet prompts
    • anatomy prompts
    • body anchors
    • general character prompts

    Blake is a 6'3" tall, muscular athletic man with a broad, powerful frame and strong upper-body development.

    His physique is defined by wide shoulders, a strong chest, and thick arms, giving him a visibly larger and more imposing presence than most people around him. His body reads as strength and stability rather than agility, with a powerful athletic silhouette that emphasizes upper-body mass and broad proportions.

    Blake’s overall presence is calm, confident, and grounded. His posture is naturally upright and controlled, and his relaxed body language conveys quiet authority and physical confidence without the need for exaggerated movement.

    Identity Block (Short)

    Use for:

    • quick prompts
    • scene prompts
    • interaction prompts

    Blake is a 6'3" tall muscular athlete with a broad-shouldered frame, strong chest, and short dark brown hair.
    His aesthetic is athletic-luxury styling with fitted clothing, dark neutral colors, and clean minimalist design.

    Identity Block (Extended)

    Use for:

    • character setup
    • detailed prompts
    • new chat initialization

    Blake is 6'3" with a tall, muscular athletic build and strong upper-body mass.

    Key facial traits:

    • face shape: square face with strong masculine structure
    • jawline: defined masculine jawline
    • eyes: calm confident eyes
    • hair: short dark brown hair with clean athletic styling
    • skin tone: light skin with a warm natural tone

    Body proportions:

    • shoulder width: broad shoulders with strong upper-body presence
    • torso shape: muscular torso with solid chest development
    • limb proportions: thick arms and strong athletic legs
    • posture: upright, grounded, and confident

    Overall silhouette:

    A tall, broad, upper-body-dominant athletic silhouette defined by wide shoulders, strong arms, and a powerful frame.

    Face Block

    Use for:

    • face anchor prompts
    • portrait prompts
    • expression sheets

    Facial description:

    • face shape: square masculine face
    • jawline: defined jawline with strong structure
    • cheekbones: moderately defined
    • eye shape: calm focused eyes
    • eyebrow style: strong natural brows
    • hairstyle framing the face: short dark brown athletic hairstyle

    Maintain consistent facial proportions and identity.

    Body Block

    Use for:

    • anatomy prompts
    • body anchor prompts
    • pose prompts
    • scale prompts

    Blake is 6'3" with a tall muscular athletic build.

    Body proportions:

    • shoulders: broad and powerful
    • torso: muscular chest and upper body
    • waist: solid athletic waist
    • hips: balanced athletic structure
    • limbs: thick arms and strong legs

    Posture:

    Upright and grounded with relaxed but controlled confidence.

    Overall silhouette should read as:

    A tall, broad, powerful athletic frame with strong upper-body dominance.

    Movement Block

    Use for:

    • pose sheets
    • motion anchors
    • dynamic scenes

    Movement style:

    Strong, deliberate movement with grounded physical presence.

    Posture tendencies:

    Upright stance with relaxed stability.

    Gesture style:

    Confident, open gestures with controlled physical movement.

    Expression Block

    Use for:

    • expression sheets
    • portraits
    • interaction scenes

    Default expression:

    Confident neutral expression with calm focus.

    Typical smile:

    Warm, slightly amused smile.

    Emotional range:

    Controlled emotional presence with steady confidence.

    Avoid exaggerated or cartoonish expressions unless explicitly requested.

    Wardrobe Description Block

    Use for:

    • outfit sheets
    • wardrobe generation
    • style prompts

    Clothing should reflect:

    Polished athletic styling that emphasizes his strong upper body.

    Common elements:

    • fitted athletic shirts or tanks
    • tapered joggers or athletic shorts
    • clean minimalist jackets

    Accessories may include:

    • athletic trainers
    • casual sneakers
    • minimal watch

    Avoid styles that conflict with the character’s core aesthetic.

    Anti-Drift Rules

    These traits must remain consistent across generated images.

    Always preserve:

    • height and body proportions
    • broad shoulders and strong chest
    • tall muscular athletic frame
    • facial structure and hairstyle silhouette
    • dark athletic clothing palette

    Avoid:

    • slim runner-style build
    • narrow shoulders
    • delicate physique

    Short Prompt Description

    Copy this block directly into short prompts.

    Blake is a 6'3" tall muscular athlete with broad shoulders, a strong chest, and short dark brown hair.
    His style combines athletic luxury with fitted clothing and dark neutral colors.

    Full Prompt Description

    Copy this block into prompts that require a full character description.

    Blake is 6'3" with a tall muscular athletic build, broad shoulders, thick arms, and short dark brown hair with a strong masculine face.
    His aesthetic includes athletic-luxury styling, fitted athletic clothing, and dark neutral colors.
    Maintain consistent facial identity, body proportions, and style across all views.
