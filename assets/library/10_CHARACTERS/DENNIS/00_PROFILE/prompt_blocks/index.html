    Prompt Blocks — Dennis

    This file contains reusable prompt fragments describing the character.

    Each block can be copied into prompts depending on the generation task.

    Character Block

    Use for:

    • reference sheet prompts
    • anatomy prompts
    • body anchors
    • general character prompts

    Dennis is a medium-height man with a soft, hip-dominant physique and a gentle overall presence. His build is naturally soft and untrained, with body mass concentrated in the abdomen, hips, glutes, and thighs, creating a clearly leg-dominant silhouette. His shoulders are moderate and his torso is soft with minimal muscular definition, while his limbs—especially the thighs—appear fuller and heavier. Dennis has a soft round face, a gentle jawline, light skin with freckles across the nose and cheeks, and medium calm eyes behind rectangular glasses. His hair is short dark brown with natural texture. His overall aesthetic is domestic_soft and soft_masculine, with relaxed casual clothing and understated everyday styling.

    Identity Block (Extended)

    Use for:

    • character setup
    • new chat initialization
    • style-sensitive prompts

    Dennis is a medium-height man with a naturally soft, untrained physique and a hip-dominant body structure. His shoulders are moderate while the hips, glutes, and thighs carry more visible mass, creating a leg-dominant silhouette with rounded contours rather than muscular definition. His torso is soft with a natural stomach contour, and his limbs—particularly the thighs—appear thicker and heavier.

    His face has a soft round shape with a gentle jawline and calm features. He has light skin with freckles across the nose and cheeks, medium eyes behind rectangular glasses, and short dark brown hair with natural texture.

    Dennis’s aesthetic is domestic_soft and soft_masculine. His clothing tends to be relaxed, comfortable, and understated, typically in navy, neutral tones, and soft earth colors.

    Body Block

His torso is soft with a natural stomach contour and minimal muscular definition. His limbs are thicker, particularly the thighs and upper legs. His posture is relaxed and neutral, with a grounded stance.

His rectangular glasses are a recurring accessory.

    Wardrobe Description Block

    Use for:

His outfits often include simple shirts, soft sweaters, casual trousers, or comfortable shorts. Clothing silhouettes are relaxed and natural rather than fitted or athletic. Materials are usually cotton or wool, and the palette commonly includes navy, neutral tones, and earth colors.

    Anti-Drift Rules

    Use for:

    • identity preservation prompts
    • difficult scenes
    • regeneration corrections

    Always preserve:

    • soft rounded facial structure with gentle jawline
    • hip-dominant body proportions with fuller hips and glutes
    • natural soft torso and stomach contour
    • rectangular eyeglasses
    • calm understated demeanor

    Avoid:

    • athletic V-shaped torso
    • exaggerated shoulder width
    • visible muscular definition or bodybuilder physique
