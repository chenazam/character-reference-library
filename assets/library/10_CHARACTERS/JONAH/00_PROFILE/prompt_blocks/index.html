    Prompt Blocks — Jonah

    This file contains reusable prompt fragments describing the character. Each block can be copied into prompts depending on the generation task.

    Character Block

    Use for:

    • reference sheet prompts
    • anatomy prompts
    • body anchors
    • general character prompts

    Jonah is a tall man (178 cm / 5'10") with a lean athletic build and naturally elongated proportions that give him a graceful, elegant presence.

    He has expressive green eyes, refined balanced facial features, and dark blonde medium-length wavy hair with soft side-swept volume.

    His silhouette reads as tall and slender with long limbs, a narrow waist, and moderately broad but balanced shoulders that emphasize an elongated athletic frame.

    His aesthetic centers on playful athletic fashion with sporty expressive clothing such as crop tops, athletic shorts, and bold casual outfits. Maintain consistent facial identity, hairstyle, body proportions, and overall silhouette across all views.

    His aesthetic is playful athletic fashion with sporty expressive clothing such as crop tops, athletic shorts, and bold casual outfits.

    Identity Block (Extended)

    Use for:

    • character setup
    • detailed prompts
    • new chat initialization

    Jonah is a tall man (178 cm / 5'10") with a lean athletic physique and naturally elongated proportions.

    Key facial traits:

    • face shape: long oval face with balanced refined features
    • jawline: defined but not overly sharp
    • eyes: expressive green eyes
    • hair: dark blonde medium-length wavy hair with soft side-swept volume
    • skin tone: light natural skin tone

    Body proportions:

    • shoulder width: moderately broad but not bulky
    • torso shape: lean athletic torso with natural muscle tone
    • limb proportions: long arms and legs creating an elongated silhouette
    • posture: relaxed confident posture with open body language

    Overall silhouette:

    A tall slender athletic silhouette with long limbs, a narrow waist, and balanced shoulders that read as elegant and elongated rather than bulky.

    Face Block

    Use for:

    • face anchor prompts
    • portrait prompts
    • expression sheets

    Facial description:

    • face shape: long oval
    • jawline: defined but smooth and balanced
    • cheekbones: moderately defined
    • eye shape: expressive almond-shaped eyes
    • eyebrow style: natural medium-density brows
    • hairstyle framing the face: medium-length dark blonde wavy hair with soft side-swept volume and loose waves

    Maintain consistent facial proportions and identity.

    Body Block

    Use for:

    • anatomy prompts
    • body anchor prompts
    • pose prompts
    • scale prompts

    Jonah is a tall man (178 cm / 5'10") with a lean athletic build.

    Body proportions:

    • shoulders: moderately broad but slim and balanced
    • torso: lean athletic torso with visible natural muscle tone
    • waist: narrow waist creating a tapered silhouette
    • hips: proportionate and slightly narrow
    • limbs: long arms and legs emphasizing height and elegance

    Posture:

    Relaxed upright posture with open confident body language.

    Overall silhouette should read as:

    A tall elongated athletic figure with long limbs, narrow waist, and balanced shoulders.

    Anti-Drift Rules

    These traits must remain consistent across generated images.

    Always preserve:

    • height and elongated body proportions
    • lean athletic build
    • expressive green eyes
    • dark blonde wavy hair with side-swept volume
    • playful athletic fashion aesthetic

    Avoid:

    • bulky bodybuilder physique
    • rugged overly masculine facial structure
    • very short hair or buzzcuts
    • conservative muted styling that removes his playful fashion identity
