    Character Summary — Luca

    Core Identity

    Name: Luca

    Height: 168 cm (5'6")

    Build Type: light athletic

    Age Appearance: early 20s

    Gender Presentation: masculine

    Visual Identity (Short Prompt Version)

    Use this block when a prompt needs a short character description.

    Luca is a short, compact, lean athletic young man with soft rounded facial features and a gentle approachable presence.
    His aesthetic blends cozy domestic softness with relaxed athletic styling.

    Visual Identity (Full Prompt Version)

    Use this block when a prompt needs a complete character description.

    Luca is a short, compact young man with a lean athletic build and relaxed natural presence.

    Key features:

    • face shape: soft round
    • jawline: defined but gentle jawline
    • eyes: grey-green eyes with a soft attentive gaze
    • hair: medium-short tousled brown hair with soft natural volume
    • skin tone: light skin tone with smooth even complexion

    Body proportions:

    • shoulder width: moderate shoulders with a narrow compact frame
    • torso shape: slim athletic torso with light muscle tone
    • limb proportions: slender limbs with agile proportions
    • posture: relaxed neutral posture with an easy natural stance

    Overall silhouette:

    A compact runner-style silhouette defined by a smaller frame, light athletic build, and agile balanced proportions that give Luca a soft but physically capable appearance.

    Style Identity

    Aesthetic keywords:

    • domestic_soft
    • athletic
    • cozy

    Typical clothing elements:

    • oversized hoodies or casual sweaters
    • soft athletic basics or loungewear
    • simple fitted casual clothing

    Typical materials:

    • cotton
    • linen

    Typical colors:

    Primary colors:

    • black
    • grey

    Accent colors:

    • white

    Accessories

    Recurring items that often appear with the character:

    • none

    Body Language

    Posture:

    Luca tends to stand with a relaxed, natural posture that reads comfortable and slightly reserved.

    Movement style:

    His movement style is relaxed and natural, with light agile steps and easy physical fluidity.

    Gesture style:

    Luca uses soft gentle gestures and tends to move in a calm, understated way.

    Expression Profile

    Default expression:

    A soft neutral expression with relaxed eyes and an open approachable demeanor.

    Typical smile:

    A warm gentle smile that appears easily and naturally.

    Emotional range:

    Luca’s emotions read openly and warmly, with an expressive but gentle emotional presence.

    Personality Snapshot

    Luca has a warm, caring personality and often acts as the emotional heart of the group.
    He is affectionate, attentive to others, and naturally nurturing, creating a sense of comfort and emotional safety for the people around him.

    Identity Preservation Rules

    These traits should never drift in generated images.

    Always preserve:

    • shorter compact height
    • lean athletic runner-style build
    • soft rounded facial structure
    • tousled brown hair silhouette
    • cozy domestic-soft aesthetic

    Avoid:

    • tall or broad body proportions
    • heavy muscular build or angular facial structure
