# Prompt Blocks — Blake

This file contains reusable prompt fragments describing the character.
Each block can be copied into prompts depending on the generation task.

---

## Character Block

Use for:

- reference sheet prompts
- anatomy prompts
- body anchors
- general character prompts

Blake is a 6'3" tall, muscular athletic man with a broad, powerful frame and strong upper-body development.

His physique is defined by wide shoulders, a strong chest, and thick arms, giving him a visibly larger and more imposing presence than most people around him. His body reads as strength and stability rather than agility, with a powerful athletic silhouette that emphasizes upper-body mass and broad proportions.

Blake’s overall presence is calm, confident, and grounded. His posture is naturally upright and controlled, and his relaxed body language conveys quiet authority and physical confidence without the need for exaggerated movement.
