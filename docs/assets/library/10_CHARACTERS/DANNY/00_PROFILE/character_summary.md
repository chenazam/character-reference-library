## Visual Identity (Full Prompt Version)

Use this block when a prompt needs a **complete character description**.

Danny is an imposing 6'4" man with an extremely broad, heavily muscular build and a grounded, physically powerful presence.

Key features:

- face shape: square
- jawline: broad masculine jawline
- eyes: light blue eyes with a steady attentive gaze
- hair: short light-brown hair with natural texture
- skin tone: light warm complexion with visible beard shadow and facial stubble

Body proportions:

- shoulder width: extremely broad, heavy shoulders
- torso shape: thick, powerful torso with strong chest and core mass
- limb proportions: thick muscular limbs with visible strength and density
- posture: grounded, stable posture that feels planted and physically secure

Overall silhouette:

A towering power-frame silhouette defined by massive shoulders, a thick chest and torso, and a dense muscular build that makes Danny visually dominate the space around him.
