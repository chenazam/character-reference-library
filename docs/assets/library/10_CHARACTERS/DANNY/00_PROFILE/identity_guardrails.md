# Identity Guardrails — Danny

These rules define traits that must remain consistent when generating images of Danny.

## Core Physical Identity

Always preserve:

- Height approximately 6'4" (193 cm)
- Extremely tall and physically dominant presence
- Broad, massive shoulders
- Thick muscular arms
- Powerful chest and upper-body mass
- Large heavy muscular frame (football linebacker physique)

Avoid:

- Slim athletic builds
- Runner-style proportions
- Narrow shoulders
- Lightweight or delicate silhouette
- Short body proportions

---

## Facial Identity

Always preserve:

- Square facial structure
- Broad masculine jawline
- Light blue eyes
- Short light-brown hair
- Visible facial stubble
- Mature masculine appearance (mid-20s)

Avoid:

- Narrow or delicate facial structure
- Soft rounded face
- Long hairstyles
- Completely clean baby-faced look
- Heavy full beards unless intentional

---

## Style Identity

Always preserve:

- Rugged utilitarian aesthetic
- Athletic masculine clothing
- Durable casual clothing
- Practical everyday styling

Typical palette:

- black
- charcoal
- dark red accents

Avoid:

- flamboyant or flashy fashion
- delicate or feminine styling
- extremely formal fashion
- overly decorative accessories

---

## Body Language

Typical movement:

- grounded
- powerful
- physically confident

Typical posture:

- upright
- stable
- protective stance

Typical presence:

- protective
- warm
- physically reassuring

Avoid:

- jittery or nervous movement
- delicate or timid gestures
- emotionally cold or intimidating demeanor
