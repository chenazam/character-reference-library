# Identity Guardrails — Hudson

These rules define traits that must remain consistent when generating images of Hudson.

## Core Physical Identity

Always preserve:

- Height approximately 6'2" (188 cm)
- Tall athletic build
- Broad shoulders
- Defined muscular torso
- Strong arms with visible muscle definition
- Balanced athletic frame (quarterback physique)

Avoid:

- Slim runner-style build
- Narrow shoulders
- Lightweight or delicate silhouette
- Extremely bulky bodybuilder proportions
- Short body proportions

---

## Facial Identity

Always preserve:

- Narrow angular facial structure
- Defined masculine jawline
- Strong cheekbone structure
- Light blue-green eyes
- Short brown hair with natural texture
- Clean-shaven or very light stubble

Avoid:

- Rounded or soft facial structure
- Wide or square face
- Very long hairstyles
- Heavy facial hair
- Soft boyish facial proportions

---

## Style Identity

Always preserve:

- Athletic luxury aesthetic
- Clean sport-influenced clothing
- Fitted clothing emphasizing shoulders and torso
- Minimalist styling

Typical palette:

- black
- white
- grey

Avoid:

- Bright playful color palettes
- Flashy or flamboyant fashion
- Oversized baggy silhouettes
- Excessive accessories
- Very rugged or workwear-heavy styling

---

## Body Language

Typical movement:

- calm
- controlled
- confident

Typical posture:

- upright
- relaxed but composed
- physically grounded

Typical presence:

- quiet authority
- confident stillness
- restrained dominance

Avoid:

- jittery or chaotic movement
- exaggerated expressive gestures
- overly submissive body language
- awkward or hunched posture
