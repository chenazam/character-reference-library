# Identity Guardrails — Jasper

These rules define traits that must remain consistent when generating images of Jasper.

## Core Physical Identity

Always preserve:

- Height approximately 5'7" (170 cm)
- Lean runner-style athletic build
- Narrow waist with light upper-body mass
- Strong runner thighs
- Prominent rounded glutes
- Leg-dominant athletic silhouette

Avoid:

- Bulky or heavy upper-body musculature
- Thick torso or bodybuilder proportions
- Large chest mass
- Heavy-set or stocky build
- Tall body proportions

---

## Facial Identity

Always preserve:

- Oval face shape
- Defined but not overly sharp jawline
- Expressive eyes
- Short light brown / dark blonde hair
- Youthful but mature late-twenties appearance

Avoid:

- Long or narrow face
- Thick facial hair
- Very short buzzcut hairstyles
- Dramatic aging

---

## Style Identity

Always preserve:

- Playful athletic aesthetic
- Very short athletic shorts
- Fitted athletic tops or tanks
- Leg-focused clothing choices

Avoid:

- Long or baggy shorts
- Heavy winter clothing covering legs
- Formal tailoring
- Bulky layered outfits

---

## Body Language

Typical movement:

- relaxed
- energetic
- agile

Typical posture:

- casual
- weight often shifted
- expressive stance

Avoid:

- rigid military posture
- heavy grounded stance
- stiff body language
