## Character Block

Use for:

- reference sheet prompts
- anatomy prompts
- body anchors
- general character prompts

Jasper is a 5'7" lean runner-built man with a compact athletic physique and strong lower-body development.

He has a leg-dominant athletic silhouette with powerful runner thighs and prominent rounded glutes, while his upper body remains lighter and more agile in appearance. His narrow waist and balanced proportions give him a naturally athletic but not bulky build that reads fast, energetic, and physically expressive.

Jasper’s overall appearance conveys youthful confidence and playful charisma. His relaxed posture, expressive body language, and easy smile create the impression of someone socially confident, athletic, and comfortable in his own body.
