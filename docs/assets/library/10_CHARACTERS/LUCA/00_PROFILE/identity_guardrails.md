# Identity Guardrails — Luca

These rules define traits that must remain consistent when generating images of Luca.

## Core Physical Identity

Always preserve:

- Height approximately 5'6" (168 cm)
- Compact lean athletic build
- Smaller frame compared to other characters
- Light athletic musculature
- Narrow waist and balanced hips
- Slender limbs with agile proportions

Avoid:

- Tall body proportions
- Broad or bulky shoulders
- Heavy muscular build
- Bodybuilder physique
- Very thin or fragile-looking frame

---

## Facial Identity

Always preserve:

- Soft round facial structure
- Gentle jawline
- Grey-green eyes
- Medium-short tousled brown hair
- Youthful early-20s appearance
- Soft approachable facial expression

Avoid:

- Sharp angular facial structure
- Square or heavy jawlines
- Very rugged or aged facial appearance
- Long hairstyles
- Heavy facial hair

---

## Style Identity

Always preserve:

- Domestic-soft aesthetic
- Cozy casual clothing
- Relaxed athletic casualwear
- Comfortable everyday clothing

Typical palette:

- black
- grey
- white

Avoid:

- Very rugged workwear styling
- Harsh militaristic clothing
- Extremely formal fashion
- Flashy or aggressive styling

---

## Body Language

Typical movement:

- relaxed
- gentle
- fluid

Typical posture:

- relaxed neutral stance
- soft natural posture
- slightly reserved presence

Typical presence:

- warm
- nurturing
- emotionally open

Avoid:

- aggressive physical presence
- stiff or rigid posture
- intimidating body language
- exaggerated dominance poses
