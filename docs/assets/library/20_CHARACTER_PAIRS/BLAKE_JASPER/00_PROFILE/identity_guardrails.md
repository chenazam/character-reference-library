# Identity Guardrails — Blake & Jasper

These rules apply when Blake and Jasper appear together.

They define the visual relationship between the characters.

---

## Height Relationship

Always preserve:

- Blake must be visibly taller than Jasper
- Height difference approximately 8 inches (20 cm)

Avoid:

- Equal height
- Jasper appearing taller than Blake

---

## Body Contrast

Always preserve:

Blake:

- larger frame
- broader shoulders
- heavier upper-body mass

Jasper:

- leaner frame
- narrower shoulders
- leg-dominant silhouette

Avoid:

- both characters having similar builds
- Jasper appearing equally muscular
- Blake appearing slim or runner-built

---

## Silhouette Contrast

Always preserve:

- Blake reads as broad and imposing
- Jasper reads as compact and agile

Avoid:

- identical body proportions
- mirrored silhouettes

---

## Clothing Contrast

Typical wardrobe contrast:

Blake:

- darker colors
- fitted athletic clothing
- minimalist styling

Jasper:

- brighter or playful colors
- very short shorts
- expressive athletic styling

Avoid:

- identical outfits
- identical color palettes
- Jasper wearing long shorts that hide his legs

---

## Body Language Dynamic

Typical interaction dynamic:

Blake:

- grounded
- protective
- relaxed confidence

Jasper:

- playful
- expressive
- socially energetic

Avoid:

- Blake appearing timid or physically smaller
- Jasper appearing physically dominant

---

## Interaction Positioning

When standing together:

- Blake typically occupies the larger physical presence
- Jasper may lean toward Blake
- Blake’s arm around Jasper’s shoulders is a common interaction pose

Avoid:

- stiff symmetrical poses
- characters standing rigidly apart
