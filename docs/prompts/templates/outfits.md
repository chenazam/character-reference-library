# Outfit and Design Templates

These templates define the **visual design language and wardrobe** of a character.

They help generate:

- signature outfits
- additional outfit variants
- wardrobe overview sheets
- design language references

This stage is less rigid than the identity-definition stages and is intended to support character-specific flexibility.
