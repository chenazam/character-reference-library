# Lucien

## Overview

Lucien is a modern-day witch in his mid-twenties with a curious mind, a sharp wit, and a relaxed indifference toward conventional morality. Gifted with innate magical ability, he practices witchcraft largely through self-study and experimentation rather than formal training. His approach to magic is intuitive and personal, shaped more by instinct and curiosity than by rigid doctrine.

While Lucien is not malicious or evil, he is comfortable exploring morally gray areas of magic. He is willing to experiment with spells that benefit his personal life, even if they bend traditional ethical boundaries. This curiosity and willingness to take risks ultimately leads to the accidental summoning of Sir Ragnar Stormborn, an immortal knight from another time.

Visually and thematically, Lucien represents the modern, curious occultist whose quiet confidence and subtle power contrast sharply with Ragnar’s imposing warrior presence.

His shoulders are narrow and gently sloped, and his torso is slim with very subtle natural tone rather than visible musculature. His chest is soft and lightly defined without pronounced pectoral development.

His abdomen is smooth and natural with no visible abdominal segmentation. His waist is narrow and lightly tapered.

His hips remain relatively narrow but are slightly softer than the waist, transitioning into notably full, rounded glutes that create a pronounced but natural curve in the silhouette. His buttocks are the fullest part of his lower body shape and remain soft and natural rather than muscular.

His arms are slim and lightly toned, and his legs are long, slender, and lightly defined without strong muscular development.

Overall, his build appears slight, soft, and agile rather than athletic or physically imposing.

He stands approximately **5'7" (170 cm)** tall.

Lucien’s face has soft but clearly mature features. His cheekbones are gently defined, his jawline is smooth, and his chin tapers slightly. His facial structure is balanced and expressive, giving him a thoughtful and approachable presence.

His hair is **dark brown and naturally curly**, worn at a short-to-medium length with soft volume and a slightly tousled texture. His hairline has a natural irregular shape without sharp angles.

His eyes are **dark brown**, often appearing observant or quietly amused. His skin tone is light with **subtle freckles across the cheeks and nose**, adding a natural softness to his appearance.

Lucien is clean-shaven and occasionally wears simple modern glasses.

## Personality

Lucien is intelligent, curious, and slightly sarcastic. He tends to follow his own instincts rather than conforming to social expectations or rigid moral codes.

He is not particularly concerned with how others perceive him. His interest in occult practices and unconventional lifestyle can sometimes make others uncomfortable, but Lucien rarely allows external judgment to influence his choices.

At his core, he is driven by curiosity and the desire to understand hidden forces. His experimentation with magic is often playful and exploratory rather than cautious or ceremonial.

Despite his willingness to experiment with morally ambiguous magic, Lucien is not cruel or malicious. His actions are usually motivated by curiosity, personal benefit, or emotional impulse rather than a desire to harm others.

---

## Magical Identity

Lucien possesses **innate magical ability**, similar to naturally gifted witches in folklore and modern fantasy narratives. However, most of his practical magical knowledge comes from personal study, experimentation, and intuition.

His magical practice often incorporates elements such as:

- grimoires and spellbooks
- candle rituals
- talismans and charms
- herbs and natural ingredients

His approach to magic is informal and personal rather than ceremonial or academic. His workspace often resembles a lived-in occult study rather than a traditional ritual chamber.

Lucien’s design reflects a blend of modern urban fashion and occult symbolism.

Common visual elements associated with him include:

- dark or neutral clothing
- simple fitted shirts and jackets
- subtle occult jewelry

His overall aesthetic combines **modern casual style with subtle mystical undertones**, reinforcing his role as a contemporary witch living in the modern world.

---

## Narrative Role

Lucien serves as the narrative catalyst for the story. His experimentation with magic ultimately leads to the accidental summoning of **Sir Ragnar Stormborn**, a legendary warrior bound by an ancient curse.

This event forces Lucien to confront the real consequences of his magical curiosity while navigating the unexpected relationship that forms between the modern witch and the ancient knight.

Together, the two characters create a strong thematic contrast:

- Lucien represents curiosity, subtlety, and modern magical intuition.
- Ragnar represents discipline, strength, and an ancient warrior tradition.

Their differences in personality, physical presence, and worldview form the foundation for their evolving dynamic.
