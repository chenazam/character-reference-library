    Jasper

    Overview

    Jasper is a shorter, compact, athletic man with a lively silhouette and a visually distinctive lower-body emphasis that makes proportion and body shape especially important to preserve. His identity depends on a consistent balance of facial recognizability, compact build, strong leg presence, and playful physical styling.

    This page is the main visual reference hub for the character and is optimized for AI image generation consistency.
